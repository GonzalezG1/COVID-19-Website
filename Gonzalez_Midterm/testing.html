Community-Based Testing Center

PCR Tests

All of IDPH's Testing Sites are FREE. IDPH recommends people who are getting tested to create their own MyChart account, and schedule their appointment. The good thing of having your own account is that you will see your results as soon as they know your result. You will get notified depending on the notification preference that you choose--email or text message. For people who do not have a smart phone or are not tech savy, the workers will schedule your appointment without creating an account so you will get your results through a phone call, and if you need a paper copy, you have to request it. The downside of not having your own account, is that it takes longer to have a paper copy since they send it through mail.
