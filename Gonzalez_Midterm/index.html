What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is a disease caused by coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2. This type of coronavirus has not been seen before. You can get COVID-19 through contact with another person who has the virus. It is predominantly a respiratory illness that can affect other organs.

The Most Common Symptons of COVID-19:

  • Fever
  • Coughg
  • Headaches
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Loss of tasye or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Nausea
  • Diarrhea

Other symptoms are signs of serious illness. If someone has trouble breathing, chest pain or pressure, or difficulty staying awake, get medical care immediately.
